Congratulations

You’ve recently said “yes” to the love of your life. Now before you say “I do”, let the wedding experts at Wedding Planner help you with the wedding planning process ahead. Wedding Planner is a wedding planning boutique offering distinctive weddings for the not so average bride. We are dedicated to making your vision a reality and creating an unforgettable event.

Elizabeth Carroll

Head Wedding Planner

Elizabeth has always had a passion for organization. Her outgoing spirit led her to the field of marketing, where her career began upon graduation from Baylor University. She started Wedding Planner in the fall of 2009 with a backyard wedding of about 40 guests. It has since grown into a blossoming boutique, producing about 25 weddings a year.

Amanda Moreno

Wedding Planner

Amanda loves to tie all the details of a wedding together. She feels it’s a privilege to share in a couple's happiest day. Amanda has eight years of wedding planning experience, helping plan events in Seattle, Portland, and all over the Lonestar State. She has her Bachelor in Marketing from the University of Texas at Tyler.

Tracy Dean

Wedding Coordinator

Brides, family, friends and vendors alike appreciate Tracy’s no-nonsense approach and helpful attitude. Her background in account management keeps her and everyone around her on task and happy to be a part of something as special as your wedding. Tracy has a BS in Journalism from the University of North Texas.

About Wedding
Planner

Based in Athens, GA, Wedding Planner offers wedding planning services that range from full service planning to month-of-coordination. Because all weddings are unique, we strive to offer each bride a unique experience that fits her wants and needs.

Since our first wedding conducted in 2007, we have always organized successful wedding ceremonies. Whether it was catering, designing floral arrangements, or planning, we've enjoyed all aspects of the wedding day. Wedding Planner is the result of years of hard work serving others on this special day. We are passionate about providing quality customer services and working directly with the brides.
