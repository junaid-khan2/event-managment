Rehearsal and Wedding
Day Services Only

We provide wedding day services only and the following are some of the services you would be provided with:

  • Consultation with bride and groom to find out information about their wedding and to determine how they wish the wedding to flow;
  • Preparation of a rehearsal and wedding day itinerary;
  • Attendance and direction at the rehearsal (up to 2 hours);
  • Checking with vendors to confirm arrangements for the wedding day;
  • Attendance at overseeing and directing the ceremony and reception (up to 10 hours).
